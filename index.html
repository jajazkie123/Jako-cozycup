About Us

Founded in 2018, CozyCup Café began as a small dream to create a sanctuary for coffee lovers in the heart of the city. We believe that a great cup of coffee is more than just caffeine—it’s a moment of peace, a spark for conversation, and a daily ritual.

We source our beans ethically from small-batch roasters, ensuring every sip supports sustainable farming. Whether you're here for your morning espresso or a quiet afternoon with a book, our baristas are dedicated to making you feel right at home.
